Snakes & Ladders Rules

  • Players: 2–4 players on a 10×10 board (squares 1–100).
  • Goal: Be the first player to reach exactly square 100.
  • Your turn: Click Roll to roll two dice: a movement die and a twist die.
  • 🪜 Ladders (green): Land on the foot of a ladder to climb straight to the top.
  • 🐍 Snakes (red): Land on a snake's head to slide down to its tail.
  • Overshoot: If your roll would take you past square 100, you don't move this turn.
  • 🎲 Double Roll: Your movement dice is rolled twice — total both values!
  • 🛡️ Shield: Grants snake immunity for your next 2 turns.
  • 🔁 Swap: Choose a player to swap board positions with.
  • 💣 Bomb: Choose a player to send back 10 squares.
  • 🔀 Chaos: All players' positions rotate clockwise.
  • ⭐ Free Move: Jump to any square within ±5 of your current position.
  • Winning: Land exactly on square 100 to win the game!
