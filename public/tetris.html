Tetris Rules

  • Goal: Clear lines by completing full rows of blocks.
  • Controls: ←→ Move • ↑ Rotate • ↓ Soft Drop • Space Hard Drop • C Hold
  • Scoring: 1 line = 100 • 2 lines = 300 • 3 lines = 500 • 4 lines (Tetris) = 800, multiplied by level.
  • Level up: Every 10 lines cleared increases the level and speed.
  • Battle: Clear 2+ lines to send garbage rows to your opponent. 2 lines = 1 garbage, 3 = 2, Tetris = 4. Combos add bonus garbage.
  • Garbage cancel: Your outgoing garbage cancels any pending incoming garbage first.
  • Modes: Marathon (endless), Sprint (40 lines), Ultra (2 min), Survival (rising speed), Dig (clear garbage).
