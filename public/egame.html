E-Game Rules

  • Players: 2 players, each with 5 cards per round.
  • Cards: Emperor side gets 1 Emperor + 4 Citizens. Slave side gets 1 Slave + 4 Citizens.
  • Hierarchy:
    • Emperor beats Citizen
    • Citizen beats Slave
    • Slave beats Emperor
    • Citizen vs Citizen = Draw
  • Gameplay: Both players pick a card simultaneously. Cards are revealed once both have chosen.
  • Rounds: After every 3 turns, sides swap. 4 rounds × 3 turns = 12 turns total.
  • Scoring: Win as Emperor = 1 point. Win as Slave = 3 points.
  • Winner: Highest score after 12 turns wins.
