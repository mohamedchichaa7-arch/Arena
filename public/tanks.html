Tank Battle Rules

  • Players: 2-4 players, each with a tank (100 HP).
  • Turns: 30 seconds per turn. Move, aim, set power, choose weapon, then fire!
  • Terrain: Destructible! Explosions carve craters. Fall off = death.
  • Wind: Changes each turn. Affects projectile trajectory.
  • Weapons:
    • 🔵 Standard Shell — unlimited, moderate damage
    • 💥 Heavy Shell — 3 shots, big boom
    • 🌀 Cluster Bomb — 2 shots, splits into 5 bomblets
    • 🎯 Sniper Round — 3 shots, straight line, ignores wind
    • ☁️ Air Strike — 1 shot, pick X position, 3 shells rain down
    • 🛡️ Shield — 1 use, absorbs next hit
  • Win: Last tank standing wins!
