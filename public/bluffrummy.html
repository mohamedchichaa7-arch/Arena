Bluff Rummy Rules

  • Setup: 52 cards dealt evenly among 2–4 players. Any 4-of-a-kind in your initial hand is auto-discarded.
  • Gameplay: On your turn, play 1–3 cards face-down to the meld pile and announce a number (e.g. "Kings").
  • Bluffing: You can lie about what you played! The cards don't have to match your announcement.
  • Challenge: After any play, the next player can challenge. Cards are revealed:
    • If it was a bluff — the bluffer takes the entire meld pile.
    • If it was honest — the challenger takes the pile.
  • 4-of-a-kind: If you collect all 4 cards of the same number, they are auto-discarded.
  • Last card: Playing your last card doesn't win immediately — the next player can still challenge!
  • Winning: First player to get rid of all their cards wins!
