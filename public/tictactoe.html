Infinite Tic Tac Toe Rules

  • Players: 2 players — one plays ✕, the other ○.
  • Turns: Players take turns placing their mark on the 3×3 grid.
  • Vanishing rule: Each player can have at most 3 pieces on the board. When you place a 4th, your oldest piece vanishes.
  • Winning: Get 3 of your marks in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal).
  • Strategy: The vanishing mechanic means you can't just block — positions change every turn!
