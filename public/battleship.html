Sea Battle Rules

  • Setup: Place your 5 ships on the grid. Ships cannot overlap.
  • Ships: Carrier (5), Battleship (4), Cruiser (3), Submarine (3), Destroyer (2).
  • Gameplay: Take turns firing at your opponent's grid. Hit = you go again. Miss = opponent's turn.
  • Sinking: A ship is sunk when all its cells are hit.
  • Winning: First player to sink all 5 enemy ships wins.
